DARG

DARG is a first-person point-and-click escape game. What makes DARG unique is its use of the real world, such as social media and the internet, to solve the game's puzzles.

Download now

My role

On this project, I was assigned as the project manager as well as a C# programmer. In this role, I had to monitor the progress of my collaborators' work, guide them toward simpler solutions to accomplish their tasks, prepare their assignments, and set deadlines to help organize their work.
Coding

This game was developed on Unity in C#. Regarding my work as a programmer on the project, I was mainly responsible for the camera: its movements, position, viewing angles, and fluidity during movement. I was also tasked with implementing and programming the entire UI of the game, as well as the various menus. I also assisted the gameplay programmer when he encountered difficulties, and finally, I participated in fixing the various bugs throughout the development process. 

Organization

On this project, there were five of us, and as the leader, it was essential for me to know my collaborators and their specializations. Once that was done, we had to brainstorm the concept of the game, with everyone participating, and then assign tasks to each team member. To facilitate collaboration, we used GitHub so that everyone could work on their tasks independently, as well as Google Drive to simplify file sharing. At the beginning of each week, I requested a progress update from my collaborators to assist them in case of difficulties or to assign them new tasks.

ARG

As mentioned in the project presentation, the uniqueness of the game lies in its interaction with external elements such as the internet and social media. I was responsible for creating various fake accounts and keeping them active to make them seem real. The main challenge of this project was ensuring the accessibility of the different accounts necessary to complete the game.
